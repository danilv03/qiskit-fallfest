About the Event

Qiskit Fall Fest is the world's largest collection of student-run quantum computing events. As one of the 50 sponsored international universities selected to host it, we bring together a collection of talented quantum enthusiasts along with IBM Quantum members to mark a celebration of the advances that quantum computing has made in the past year.

With experienced quantum scientists and budding young quantum enthusiasts collaborating to showcase their talents at the workshops and hackathons at the University of Sharjah campus, we welcome you to the event and are hoping to see you there!

GDG Club

Introducing GDG Club, the pioneering Quantum Computing Special Team at University of Sharjah. GDG Club is a dynamic force driven by passionate college students, charting new territories in the ever-expanding world of quantum computing.

IBM Qiskit

Qiskit is an open-source framework developed by IBM for quantum computing. It allows users to create and run quantum programs on both simulators and real quantum hardware. Join us极速 to learn and experiment with this cutting-edge technology.
