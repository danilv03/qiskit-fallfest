About the Fest

Welcome to Qiskit Fall Fest 2025

Welcome to Qiskit Fest Fall 2025, an exciting event hosted by the students of the University of Sharjah in collaboration with IBM Quantum. We are joining IBM's global initiative to make quantum computing accessible to all through the powerful Qiskit framework.

This is your opportunity to dive into the latest developments, witness groundbreaking discoveries, and understand the real-world applications of quantum technology. We are building a community of future leaders and innovators, and we want you to be a part of it. This event is open to all students, regardless of your academic background or technical skills. If you are ready to explore the frontiers of science and technology, this event is for you!
